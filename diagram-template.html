Grid diagram — inline expandable boxes

Boxes snap to a cell grid; arrows auto-route. A box can hold its own canvas — click ⤢ to expand it in place: it enlarges to show its contents at full size and pushes the other boxes aside. Multiple boxes open at once, recursively (⤢ Expand all), so you can see the whole hierarchy on one canvas. ⤡ collapses. Toggle Edit to draw/resize boxes and arrows.
